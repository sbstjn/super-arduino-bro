Super Arduino Bro

Sexy pixel Jump'n'Run for Arduino with 8x8 RGB LED Matrix

Super cute and awesome pixel Jump'n'Run for your Arduino with an 8x8 RGB LED matrix. Download the game, wire up three buttons (for left, right, jump) and use the iTead Studio Colors Shield for connecting the LED matrix to your Arduino. Have fun with Super Arduino Bro!
